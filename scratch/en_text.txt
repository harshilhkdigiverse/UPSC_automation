(4)With reference to the severe heatwave and water crisis in New Delhi during June 2024, consider the following statements: 

1.Urban Heat Island effect intensifies heatwaves in metropolitan cities.
2.Over-extraction of groundwater is a major cause of urban water stress. 
3.Heat Action Plans are legally mandated under the Disaster Management Act, 2005. 

Which of the statements given above are correct?
(a) 1 and 2 only 
(b) 2 and 3 only
(c) 1 and 3 only
(d) 1, 2 and 3
Answer:(a)

Solution:
1) Detailed Content & Why Other Options Are Wrong Statement 1 is correct because urban areas with dense construction, asphalt roads, and limited vegetation trap heat,increasing both daytime and night-time temperatures.Statement 2 is correct as New Delhi heavily depends on groundwater beyond sustainable recharge limits.Statement 3 is incorrect because Heat Action Plans are policy frameworks recommended by NDMA; they are not legally mandated under the Disaster Management Act, 2005.
2) Aptitude (Clues) UPSC often makes statements incorrect by using words like “legally mandated” or “statutory” when the provision is only advisory.
3) PYQ Linkages UPSC Prelims 2019: Urban Heat Island effect UPSC Prelims 2020: Causes of groundwater depletion
4) Current Affairs Linkages (Exact Examples) In June 2024, Delhi recorded temperatures near 49°C, faced acute water shortages, tanker dependence, and judicial intervention on water allocation.
